Souta Yamashiro

Bio

I am a master student at Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing Laboratory, Department of Computer Science, School of Computing, Tokyo Institute of Technology. My research interests lie in natural language processing, especially zero anaphora resolution. I am also interested in information retrieval and text summarization.
